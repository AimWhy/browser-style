Sun Phase

Shows the sun's position across a bell-like curve, with sunrise and sunset intersecting with a baseline.

Day
<sun-phase sunrise="06:00" sunset="19:00" time="13:30">

Rising
<sun-phase sunrise="06:00" sunset="19:00" time="19:00">

Setting
<sun-phase sunrise="06:00" sunset="19:00" time="19:00">
